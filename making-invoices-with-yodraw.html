How to Make Invoices in Seconds in Yo! Draw with AI

Learn how to make invoices in Yo! Draw with script created using AI. It takes only seconds to make complete invoice in Yo! Draw (https://yodraw.com).

Step 1: Open yodraw.com.

Step 2: Click "Open App" link (app)

Step 3: Paste the prompt.

Step 4: Give details and when code is ready, click "Copy" button

Step 5: Click on Yo! Bar command pallet in Yo! Draw.

Or Press `Ctrl + /` or `CMD + /` on mac.

Step 6: Type "!" in "Type a command or search..."

Step 7: Paste the code.

Step 8: Press Enter.

Step 9: Your invoice is ready. Edit it according to needs. Export when it is according to your needs.
